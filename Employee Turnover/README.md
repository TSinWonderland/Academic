
This project focuses on predicting employee turnover using machine learning and HR analytics. By analyzing factors such as job satisfaction, performance evaluations, workload, and tenure, the model helps identify employees who are at risk of leaving the company. The workflow includes exploratory data analysis, employee clustering, handling class imbalance with SMOTE, and training multiple models with cross‑validation. A Gradient Boosting model was selected as the best performer, and employees are categorized into risk zones to support targeted, data‑driven retention strategies.
